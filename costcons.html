Cost of Construction Report is an engineer-certified document that provides detailed item-wise cost of building your house or commercial property in Puducherry. Why You Need Cost of Construction Report in Puducherry?

Purpose Requirement
Bank Construction Loan Banks in Puducherry release loan in stages based on engineer-certified cost of construction. Mandatory for loan disbursement.
Income Tax - Section 54/54F To claim capital gains exemption, you must submit Chartered Engineer certificate showing construction cost with bills.
Wealth Tax Assessment Income Tax Department requires certified cost report for properties under construction or recently completed.
Insurance Claims Insurance companies need engineer-certified reconstruction cost for claim settlement after damage.
Budget Planning Know exact cost before starting construction. Avoid fund shortage and cost overruns during project.
⚠️ Important: As per Income Tax Act, if you sell a property and invest in new construction to save capital gains tax, you must submit Chartered Engineer certificate for cost of construction. Self-made estimates are not accepted.

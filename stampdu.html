What is Stamp Duty Valuation in Puducherry?

Stamp Duty Valuation is the official assessment of a property's market value for the purpose of calculating stamp duty and registration charges payable to Puducherry Government. As per Registration Act, you must pay stamp duty based on guideline value or market value, whichever is higher. Our certified report helps you avoid undervaluation notices and penalties from Puducherry Sub-Registrar.

⚠️ Important: As per Section 47-A of Indian Stamp Act, if Puducherry Sub-Registrar finds property undervalued, they can refer to Puducherry District Collector for correct valuation. This delays registration by 3-6 months. Get proper valuation report first.
